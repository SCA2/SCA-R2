WH01 Wiring Harness

The WH01 is an assembled wiring harness that simplifies the task of connecting your modules to the PS03 power supply. Fabricated with stranded copper wire and high-reliability MOLEX contacts, the WH01 saves time and greatly reduces the chance of errors during final assembly of your SCA kits.

Features
Individual Wire Colors Each conductor in the harness has a unique color that makes voltage and continuity checks easy.
Locking Housings 6-position MOLEX housings incorporate locking ramps to maintain secure connections.
Integrated Ferrite Ferrite inductor installed between first and second connectors reduces high-frequency electrical noise.
Three-Leaf Contacts MOLEX "trifurcon" crimp contacts grip mating posts on three sides to ensure excellent long-term connectivity.

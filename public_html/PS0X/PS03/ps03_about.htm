PS03 Power Supply

The PS03 is a high-efficiency, triple-output regulated power supply designed especially for use with SCA preamp modules. The PS03 will work with any AC mains voltage, requires no jumpers, and is compatible with all of our current modules and the CH01 chassis.

Features
Universal Input The PS03 can operate on AC voltages from 85 to 250 volts, 50 or 60 Hz, and requires no jumpers.
High Voltage +/-30VDC rails allow for maximum headroom.
High Power The PS03 can provide more than 80W of output power and can easily drive 8 modules in any combination.
Low Noise High frequency switching design eliminates any possibility of audible hum. State of the art soft recovery rectifiers, RC snubbers, and dual-stage LC filters on each output minimize switching noise.
LED Health Indicators Visual indication of +30V, -30V, and +48V output voltage.

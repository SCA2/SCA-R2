Ordering Instructions
US Orders
  • To place an order using our shopping cart, please follow these simple steps:

    1. Enter the quantity and click "add" for each item you want to order. Click "Reset" to clear all entries.
    2. Enter a valid zip code and click "Get Rates" to retrieve rate information from UPS.
    3. Select your preferred shipping method. Faster service options may reduce your order's time in transit but won't make it ship any sooner.
    4. Click "Checkout" to complete your order through PayPal.
    5. If you prefer to pay by mail, print the page and send it with check or money order to:

      Seventh Circle Audio
      1714 Franklin St, Ste B1
      Oakland, CA 94612
      USA

  • Please allow two weeks for checks to clear.
  • We charge 9 3/4% tax for sales within California.
  • We ship via UPS only, no exceptions.
International
Orders
    Customers outside the US have two options. Have us ship directly via UPS, or use a forwarding service such as Shipito, MyUS, or Bongo. Direct shipping will be a bit faster; the forwarding service may be less expensive. We highly recommend using a forwarding service if you plan to make several orders to fill up your chassis. The accumulated savings can be substantial.

  • To place an order using a forwarding service:

    1. Sign up at a site such as such as Shipito, MyUS, or Bongo.
    2. Obtain a shipping address from the forwarding service. If you have a choice, get one in Oregon to avoid sales tax.
    3. Using the shopping cart, enter the quantity and click "add" for each item you want to order.
    4. Enter the ZIP code you received from the forwarding service and click "Get Rates" to retrieve rate information from UPS.
    5. Select your preferred shipping method. We recommend ground shipping.
    6. Click "Checkout" to complete your order through PayPal.
    7. We ship your order to the forwarding service, they ship it to you.


  • To place an international order for direct shipping via UPS:

    1. Using the shopping cart, enter the quantity and click "add" for each item you want to order.
    2. Open the UPS Time and Cost Calculator.
    3. Select "Detailed Time and Cost".
    4. Enter "United States", "Oakland" and "94612" as the "Ship From" information.
    5. Enter your "Ship To" information.
    6. Select the proper "Destination Type".
    7. Select a shipping date.
    8. Set "Number of Packages" to 1.
    9. For "Customs Value", enter the merchandise subtotal calculated by the shopping cart.
    10. Select "My Packaging".
    11. For "Package Weight", enter the total weight calculated by the shopping cart.
    12. If your order includes a chassis or more than 3 modules, enter "24 x 12 x 6" for package dimensions. Otherwise, enter "12 x 9 x 6".
    13. For "Declared Value", enter the merchandise subtotal calculated by the shopping cart.
    14. Select "I will prepay my shipping charges".
    15. Choose your desired UPS service.
    16. Use the "Send Money" feature of your PayPal account to send the total directly to sales@seventhcircleaudio.com. Please include a note with your payment that includes:

      • Items in your order
      • Shipping address
      • UPS service desired (Standard, Saver, etc.)
      • Telephone number

    17. If you prefer to pay by mail, send an international money order to:

      Seventh Circle Audio
      1714 Franklin St, Ste B1
      Oakland, CA 94612
      USA

  • We require a $200 USD minimum for orders outside the U.S. and Canada.
  • You are responsible for paying VAT and duties upon receipt of merchandise.
  • We will not under any circumstances alter invoices or other documents in order to lower your taxes.
  • You must include a phone number in addition to your shipping address.
  • We ship via UPS only, no exceptions.

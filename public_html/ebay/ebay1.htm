Mic Preamp Modules

C84 Preamp Module Our most neutral sounding module, the C84 is our version of the Cohen "double-balanced" preamp circuit. With performance similar to preamps from Millennia, Sytek, Grace, and other very high quality transformer-less circuits, the C84 uses a combination of discrete transistors and IC amplifiers to deliver a clean, uncolored version of your source signal. THD+N can measure as low as 0.0007%, with EIN numbers approaching theoretical limits. Tremendous headroom is available with maximum output levels as high as +32dBu, with as much as 132dB of S/N available at low gains.

Power Supply and Chassis

PS03 Power Supply A sophisticated switching supply designed specifically for audio, the PS03 can deliver more than 80W of power to your preamp modules. Compatible with all of our current modules and the CH01 chassis. Features include jumper-less universal AC input, over-current and over-temperature protection, two-stage LC output filters, and LED health indicators on each output rail. A single PS03 can easily power eight modules in any combination. When ordered with a CH01 chassis, the PS03 will arrive already installed.
CH01 Chassis Rugged steel chassis built to give a lifetime of service. Made from aluminum-clad cold-rolled steel for excellent shielding from external noise sources. Features include welded seams, internal mu metal shielding, powder coat finish, and PEM nut fasteners. Includes pre-wired lamp and switch assemblies, so no crimping or soldering is required. Shown fully loaded with 8 modules - only 2 modules are included in this auction. Hole plugs are included for unused positions.
WH01 Wiring Harness Connect your power supply and up to 8 modules with this prefabricated wiring harness. Made with 22 AWG stranded wire and MOLEX connectors, the WH01 makes chassis wiring easy and reliable. Includes ferrite core between first and second connectors for additional noise suppression. Compatible with all of our current modules and the PS03 power supply.

OneShot 1/4U Chassis

Introducing the rugged, compact, convenient, and affordable OneShot 1/4U chassis. Designed primarily for easy tabletop operation, the OneShot can also be rack mounted easily. Simply remove the feet and screw up to four OneShot chassis directly to a Raxxess UNS-1 as shown below. No other hardware required!

Features
* Accommodates any Seventh Circle Audio module
* Discrete, class-A instrument DI with high-impedance JFET input
* Separate balanced DI output
* DI output drives preamp input transformer for maximum "color"
* Relay-switched 20dB input pad
* Relay-switched microphone input impedance
* Rugged 18 gauge steel and aluminum construction
* Laser-engraved front panel
* External 24VAC power supply for minimum noise and low cost
* Includes rubber feet, wall transformer, and universal hardware kit
* Made in USA
Raxxess UNS-1 Universal Shelf not included.
